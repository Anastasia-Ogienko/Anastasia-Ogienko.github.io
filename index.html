PHOTOGRAPHY IS HONEST
aboutALETEIA is a photostudio which is concentrated on the black&white photos. Two years ago we were dreaming to create something totally different and eye-catching. All of us were huge fans of the black&white film and digital pictures, so we decided to establish our first studio. Working with our professional photographers is highly comfortable because each of us wants to make the process less stressful, amusing and fascinating. At the end of the photography session you will go out full of satisfactory emoutions and feelings.
